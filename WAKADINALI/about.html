About us

Our Story

Wakadinali's journey began on the streets of Nairobi, where Scar and Domani honed their skills through freestyle battles and underground cyphers. Our music reflects the struggles, dreams, and realities of urban Kenya, capturing the essence of life in the city with poignant lyricism and hard-hitting beats.

Musical Style

At the heart of Wakadinali's music lies a commitment to authenticity. Our beats are characterized by nostalgic boom-bap rhythms and minimalist production, allowing our lyrical prowess to shine through. Each track is a testament to our dedication to hip-hop as a form of artistic expression and resistance against social injustices.

Impact and Legacy

Our impact extends beyond our music. We have inspired a new generation of Kenyan artists to embrace our roots and challenge the status quo through our uncompromising approach to hip-hop. Our performances at local venues and music festivals electrify audiences, showcasing our dynamic stage presence and ability to captivate listeners with our powerful storytelling.
